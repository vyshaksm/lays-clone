Instagram Post: This Lay’s Dill Pickle Dip is kinda a big dill. Recipe below INGREDIENTS: 8 oz. Cream Cheese, softened ¾ cup Sour Cream ¼ cup Sweet Onion, chopped fine 1 cup Dill Pickles, chopped fine 3 Tbsp. Pickle Juice ½ tsp. Garlic Powder ½ tsp. Dill, dried 1 ½ Tbsp. Ranch Dip Seasoning Salt & Pepper to taste Fresh dill for garnish 1 bag Lay’s Kettle Cooked potato chips DIRECTIONS In a medium bowl, add cream cheese, sour cream, onion, diced pickles and pickle juice, garlic powder, dill, and ranch dip seasoning. Mix well to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cover and refrigerate for 4 hours, or until chilled. Garnish with fresh dill + serve with a bag of Lay’s Kettle Cooked potato chips. Dip & enjoy! (opens a new window)
